ABOUT

Killer of PEPE (KOP) is a force of resistance and transparency, aimed at fighting scams and malicious activities in the crypto world. Based on the iconic meme character Pepe, KOP represents a challenge and exposure of the bad actors within the crypto space through a reimagined attire.

Our goal is clear: to create a space where scams and malicious behavior are not tolerated, and to promote the values of transparency and accountability. Through the power of meme culture, KOP has become a symbol of defiance, inspiring the community to fight back and restore trust and security in the crypto world.
